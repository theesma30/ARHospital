ABOUT US

Ambattur Rotary Hospital – Quality Healthcare for All

Ambattur Rotary Hospital is proudly run by the Rotary Club of Ambattur, a dedicated organization committed to serving the community through quality healthcare and charitable initiatives.
With a mission to provide affordable and accessible healthcare to all, the hospital has grown into a trusted medical institution since its establishment in 1988.
The hospital operates under the guidance and leadership of Trustee Rtn. Ramachandran, whose vision and commitment continue to drive excellence in medical care and social service.

A Special Initiative
Free Treatment for Cerebral Palsy Kids

Ambattur Rotary Hospital is committed to community service and provides free treatments for children with cerebral palsy.

Dialysis Unit

In collaboration with TANKER Foundation (Tamilnad Kidney Research Foundation), our Dialysis Unit operates 23 dialysis machines in two shifts, offering free or subsidized dialysis treatment to patients suffering from renal failure.
